Aubrey Kornelis

Aubrey is a passionate creative who enjoys helping others bring their vision to life. Be it as a Costume Designer, Wardrobe Supervisor, Set Costumer, Costume and/or Prop Fabricator or Stylist, Aubrey is always ready to think outside the box and get her hands dirty. She has a love of textiles, distressing fabrics, gore, sci-fi, historical fashions and the use of sustainable styling when she designs. Aubrey’s all about working in collaboration and flexibility to achieve the most fabulous and innovative costumes for her clients.

Aubrey has currently worked on award winning and nominated short films such as Keys to Life directed by Kent Loomer and Lane 9 co-directed by Jessica and Richard Valentine. Aubrey has also worked as Costume Designer for feature films like Bloody Mary. The Mirrored Witch with such actors as Cory Monteith, Jason Simmons and Matt Borlen. Aubrey’s one of a kind anti-villain suit created for the web series Dark Darkness and directed by Wilson Large is one of her pride and joys. Aubrey has also had the honor to costume for stage, commercial, music videos, and styled shoots. When she is not creating for others Aubrey tries to find time to build costumes and props for her own Cosplays or haunted productions.
